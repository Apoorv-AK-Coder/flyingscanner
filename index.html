FAQs


1. What is Faresmatch NZ?

It is a travel booking platform that allows you to find cheap flight tickets in New Zealand. We are a travel reservations platform, and we have a team of experts who can help you find the most affordable deals for your reservations.

2. How do you book cheap flight tickets in New Zealand?

Finding the best flight deals in New Zealand, but not sure where to find them, means Faresmatch New Zealand is the right website for you to grab the most cost-effective deals. There are so many offers available here for booking cheap tickets.

3. Which is the cheapest day to find tickets at an affordable price?

Tuesday is a low-price day because the supply is more and demand is less on this day. This is a weekday, and most of the travelers don't arrive or depart on this day.

4. Can I find a last-minute flight booking in New Zealand?

Yes, you can find and book the New Zealand last-minute flight tickets at Faresmatch because we are a team of travel experts, and here you can not only book the last-minute cheap tickets but also get amazing discounts.

5. What is the best time to visit New Zealand?

August and September are the cheapest months to save more and the low-season time to visit New Zealand. This is the time when the crowd is less, and that's why there are high chances to grab the best deals.

6. Are weekends good to fly internationally from New Zealand?

Well, weekends are suitable for flying internationally, but not in terms of budget, because weekends are expensive to fly, and on the other hand, weekdays are cost-effective for saving more.

7. Does using incognito or private browsing mode help find cheaper flights?

No, this is a significant misconception. Airlines and booking sites use complex algorithms based on real-time demand and seat availability, not your browser cookies or search history.

8. Are one-way tickets cheaper than a round-trip?

Well, it depends on route because for domestic flights, booking two-way tickets can sometimes be cheaper. For international flights, a round-trip ticket with same airlines is often the more economical option.

9. How important is flexibility for getting cheap fares?

Yes, flexibility is the significant advantage. If you can be flexible with your travel dates, times and even alternative airports, it is a great way to save more for the reservation.

10. Are budget airlines always the cheapest option?

Not all the time because the base fare on budget airlines is low, they often charge extra for everything, including checked baggage, carry-on bags, seat selection, and even printing a boarding pass at the airport. Always check the total cost, including these extras, to compare the right one.
