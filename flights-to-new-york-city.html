Route Details of New York


Airlines

Domestic (within the U.S.): Major domestic carriers include American Airlines, Delta Air Lines, United Airlines, JetBlue, Alaska Airlines, Southwest Airlines, Spirit Airlines, and Frontier Airlines.

International: New York is a central international hub served by many airlines. The most prominent international carriers include British Airways, Air France, Lufthansa, Emirates, Qatar Airways, Cathay Pacific, Singapore Airlines, Air India, and many more.


Airports

Three major airports serve New York City:

  1. John F. Kennedy International Airport (JFK): Located in Queens, JFK is the largest and busiest airport in the New York City area, handling most international flights. It is a hub for American Airlines and Delta Air Lines.
  2. LaGuardia Airport (LGA): Also in Queens, LaGuardia is the closest airport to Manhattan and primarily serves domestic destinations, with some flights to Canada and the Caribbean.
  3. Newark Liberty International Airport (EWR): In Newark, New Jersey, EWR is a central hub for United Airlines and serves domestic and international destinations.

An alternative airport to consider, especially if you are looking for potentially cheaper flights and are willing to travel a bit further, is:

New York Stewart International Airport (SWF): Located about 60 miles north of Manhattan.


Cheapest Time to Fly

The cheapest month to fly to New York City is January to March. These three months are the most popular and ideal time to fly for this city because this is the low-season time to travel in the busiest cities of the United States. You can also check and compare fares for these months. On the other hand, the most expensive months to fly are May, June, and July, and December is also the busiest time to fly because of the New Year.


Cheapest Days of the Week

For domestic flights, the cheapest days to fly for New York are Tuesday, Wednesday, and Saturday. On the other hand, for the international flights, weekdays are generally cheaper than weekends. Cheapest time to fly is often at night.
